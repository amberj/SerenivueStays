ORIGIN: french locution
    1. at one's own place

A beautiful French idiom that evokes the comfort and belonging you feel in your own home. It's essence is "a familiar space in an unfamiliar place" or "freedom to be yourself without reservation".

Price

Minimum booking duration: 3 days (mandatory).

For Direct Bookings: INR (Indian Rupees) 4200 per night (for stays less than 7 days).

Planning to stay for long term? I offer discounts for longer stays (based on stay duration as well as amenities needed). Please connect with me on Whatsapp Chat so that we can discuss the same. 

Important points to keep in mind:

  • You can pay via UPI (if you are from India) or via Paypal (if you are based outside India). Cash payments are not accepted.
  • There is no security deposit, but 100% of amount needs to be paid before checkin.
  • Refunds and cancellations: No cancellation and no refunds on direct bookings via this website. If you want a refundable booking, please book via Airbnb as the Airbnb listing for this house has an option for refundable booking (with Airbnb taking charge of enforcing the cancellation policy as well as process refunds).

Availability

Due to proximity of this flat to some key work centers (Bagmane Tech Park, Godrej Center, Embassy Golf Links, Manipal Hospital to name a few), most of our guests end up extending their stay in this property depending on their work commitments. 

You can message me on Whatsapp with your check-in and checkout dates and I will share availability with you.
