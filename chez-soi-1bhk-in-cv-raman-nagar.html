Chez Soi (1BHK in CV Raman Nagar)

chez soi /ʃeɪ swɑː/
ORIGIN: french locution
    1. at one's own place
    2. at home
A beautiful French idiom that evokes the comfort and belonging you feel in your own home. It's essence is "a familiar space in an unfamiliar place" or "freedom to be yourself without reservation".
