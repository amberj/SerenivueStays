Chez Enclave (Private Room+Bathroom in a Shared Apartment in Indiranagar)

Chez Enclave /ʃeɪ ɒnˈkleɪv/
ORIGIN: french-english compound
    1. within our circle
    2. among our home
A beautiful French idiom that evokes the comfort and belonging you feel in living with your family in your home. It's essence is "you're not a guest, you're one of us" or "you need no invitation."
