Launching soon: Chez Nous (Private Room+Bathroom in a Shared Apartment in Indiranagar)

chez nous /ʃeɪ nu/
ORIGIN: french locution
    1. at our place
    2. at home
A beautiful French idiom that evokes the comfort and belonging you feel in living with your family in your home. It's essence is "you're not a guest, you're one of us" or "family away from home".
